    What's the story behind that Wacky Einstein Tongue Photo??

    10 Facts About Albert Einstein

    1. Before relocating to the United States in 1933, Albert Einstein resided in Germany, Italy, Switzerland, and Czechia (which was then a part of the Austro-Hungarian Empire).
    2. When Albert was a little boy, his father gave him a compass, and Albert immediately fell in love with science.
    3. Albert quit the grammar school he attended as a teenager at the age of 15 because he detested the stringent rules.
    4. Nevertheless, at the age of 16, he wrote his first scholarly paper! His compass inspired the paper, which discussed the force of magnetism.
    5. Rather than directly becoming a physicist, Albert first trained as a teacher.
    6. Albert decided to pursue a Ph.D. in physics after failing to find work as a math and physics teacher.
    7. Albert announced his famous formula, E=mc2, in 1905 as one of his discoveries.
    8. Albert's 'general theory of relativity,' which he worked on for the next ten years, included the formula.
    9. Albert's theory of relativity aided scientists in understanding the workings of the universe.
    10. In 1921, he was awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics.
